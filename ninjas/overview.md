# Overview

### 🥷 Ninjas Goals

* <mark style="color:orange;">**Basic Computer Use:**</mark> Use the mouse, keyboard, passwords, and the internet confidently.
* <mark style="color:blue;">**Core Programming Concepts:**</mark> Understand loops, conditions, and variables.
* <mark style="color:orange;">**Computer Literacy:**</mark> Solid foundation in computer skills, essential computer parts & terminology.
* <mark style="color:blue;">**Independent Project Building:**</mark> Complete a mini project i.e. Rock-Paper-Scissors or sensor app.
* <mark style="color:orange;">**Digital Problem-Solving:**</mark> Build resilience & autonomy to tackle digital challenges.
* <mark style="color:blue;">**Community & Values:**</mark> Feel part of ReDI, understand inclusion and teamwork.

### Content Roadmap

The Content Roadmap is a living document with **goals and sub-goals for each session**, helping us understand the bigger picture of what students should achieve by the end of the semester, while keeping each class connected to the overall learning journey.
