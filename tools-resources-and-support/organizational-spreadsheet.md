# Organizational Spreadsheet

This document contains:&#x20;

* **Teaching team spreadsheet:** this is where you can mark your availability and where we’ll organize the teaching teams for each Sunday.&#x20;
* **Drive with last semester’s content (for reference):** includes slides and Kahoot links.
* **Drive for this semester:** please use this folder to save any new content you create. Feel free to upload any additional materials you prepare here.
* **Credentials:** for all the important accounts.
* **Students attendance list:** please support us by taking attendancance of the kids each session.&#x20;

### <mark style="color:orange;">Not available anymore?</mark>

If you can’t attend a class, please let the team know as soon as possible:

1. Post in Slack: Tag @channel in your group Slack and ask for a substitute.
2. If no reply the same day: Contact the ReDI team (Tatiana Michan Hoyos) directly via Slack or email.
3. Planned absences: If you already know you’ll miss a class, inform Tatiana at least 3 days in advance (Mon–Fri). This gives us enough time to arrange coverage.
4. Emergencies only: In urgent situations, you may call Tatiana directly +49 16 29 07 16 57.
