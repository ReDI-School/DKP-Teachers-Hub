# 🌟 Commitment

&#x20;**What is the expected commitment for volunteers?**

* Minimum 3-4 Sundays per semester
* Bi-weekly planning call (30 minutes) to coordinate roles, if you’re joining any of the two consecutive Sundays
* Participation in Demo Day at the end of the semester
* Occasional feedback sessions and social events

**Each class requires:**

* Arrival at 11:30 (class runs 12:00–14:30) to help with the room set up
* Attendance tracking
* After the session, short feedback on Slack. You will receive an automatic message on Slack for this.
