### Meet & Greet

**Spring Semester:** January | **Fall Semester:** July

This friendly one-on-one interview is part of our volunteer admission process. It helps us understand your experience, motivations, and availability, and gives you space to ask questions. We use it to check if your profile aligns with our course needs for the upcoming semester.

**Before the call, please review:**

* About ReDI, DCP & Volunteer expectations
* Class formats, schedules & roles of the courses in your preferred track:
  * [<mark style="color:orange;background-color:orange;">**Full Stack Track**</mark>](/broken/pages/kMNZGOpLHrPliiSBFN2O)
  * [<mark style="color:orange;background-color:$danger;">**Data /**</mark> <mark style="color:orange;">**AI Track**</mark>](/broken/pages/5yVS94PkIxqSTaVOlHH2)
  * [<mark style="color:orange;">**UXUI Design**</mark>](/broken/pages/DBhf42Ao7HkCT1L8rV5z)
* Semester Timeline and key dates
* Your availability

Teaching experience is a plus but not required. What matters most is your availability, commitment, responsibility to the teaching team, and motivation to keep learning and support students.

_Interested? Book your Meet & Greet Call now!_

### Admission

**Spring Semester Deadline:** End of January | **Fall Semester Deadline :** July

We review each profile based on your interests and skills to ensure a good match with the needs of our Teaching Teams. Once your participation is confirmed, you’ll receive an email with your course assignment and be asked to verify your availability promptly. After that, you’ll gain access to our Self-Onboarding process with all the tools and materials you’ll need.\
If no spots are available, we’ll contact you for future volunteer opportunities and add you to our _Waiting List._
