# Overview

### 🦸🏻‍♂️ Heroes Goals

* <mark style="color:orange;">**Computer Confidence:**</mark> Enhance computer skills, use shortcuts, and navigate confidently.
* <mark style="color:blue;">**Fun with Scratch:**</mark> Learn Scratch through engaging, playful projects.
* <mark style="color:orange;">**Programming Concepts:**</mark> Deepen understanding of loops, conditions, variables, and events.
* <mark style="color:blue;">**Creative Design:**</mark> Create characters and scenes for animations and games.
* <mark style="color:orange;">**Teamwork & Communication:**</mark> Build problem-solving, collaboration, and presentation skills.
*   <mark style="color:blue;">**Community & Values:**</mark> Feel part of ReDI, understand inclusion and teamwork.



### Content Roadmap
