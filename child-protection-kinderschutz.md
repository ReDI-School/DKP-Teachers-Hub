# 📜 Child Protection / Kinderschutz

In Germany, Kinderschutz (child protection) refers to laws and measures that safeguard the safety, rights, and well-being of children. At ReDI, this is a top priority, and all volunteers are expected to follow these principles:

* Background checks: All volunteers must provide a criminal record certificate (erweitertes Führungszeugnis). This cannot be&#x20;
* Reporting obligations: If you suspect abuse, neglect, or harm, inform the ReDI team immediately at [kids@redi-school.org](mailto:kids@redi-school.org).
* Informed consent: Always ensure parental consent for participation in activities and for taking pictures of children.

Child protection means creating a safe, respectful, and supportive environment for every student.
