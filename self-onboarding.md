# 🚀 Self-Onboarding



{% hint style="danger" %}
If you are a returning teacher who was on a break during the last semester, please feel free to skip the steps outlined in this section and continue directly with the [Volunteer Agreement Process.](https://redi-school-1.gitbook.io/teacher-hub/self-onboarding/volunteer-agreement)
{% endhint %}

Here's an overview of the key tools we use for teaching and communication. Please set up these tools **now** to ensure effective collaboration with your team and students.

#### ✅ **Have you read all the information on this website?** <a href="#slack" id="slack"></a>

Please make sure you go through all the content in the **Teachers’ Hub** carefully. If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, feel free to contact the Volunteer Coordinator at [tatiana@redi-school.org](mailto:tatiana@redi-school.org). We’re here to support you every step of the way! 🚀

#### ✅ Slack <a href="#slack" id="slack"></a>

Slack is ReDI School's **main communication channel**, as a volunteer teacher you are asked to check it out regularly and be active. You'll receive an invitation link to our Slack channel as soon as you finish this onboarding. &#x20;

**What can be found on Slack?**&#x20;

* Course-specific communication
* Organizational Spreadsheet
* Updates / info regarding classes & events
* Weekly class feedback

**What should be communicated on Slack?**

* Any change in your availability&#x20;
* Any subject you want to discuss with the other teachers
* Direct message to the Volunteer Coordinator if you have any issues to discuss with the ReDI team, we are here for you.

#### **✅ Know your Rights**

* You are insured while working at ReDI.
* You can ask for a recommendation letter.&#x20;
* Always have the right to feel safe and supported, and that your work is respected.
* You have the right to be trained in teaching skills.
* You can change, amend and negotiate the class rules with your students.
* You don’t have to deal with every situation on your own.
* Please reach out to us if you need any kind of  support, regarding a student in your group who may be falling behind, technical set-up, content ideas etc. You name it, we are here for you!&#x20;

#### ✅ Do you have access to everything? <a href="#do-you-have-access-to-everything" id="do-you-have-access-to-everything"></a>

Please complete this step to receive your **Volunteer Agreement**. Once received, make sure to sign it in order to become an official volunteer.

**What is the Volunteer Agreement?**&#x20;

The Volunteer Agreement is a set of mutual understandings between ReDI School and the volunteer. We hope that this agreement will allow you to feel comfortable with us. The purpose of this agreement is to create a safe environment in which to perform your role, to ensure respect for your privacy, including keeping your private information confidential and to mutually agree on behaviour that adheres to your rights as a volunteer as well as our students’ rights. Please don’t hesitate to ask questions. Your volunteer coordinator will gladly answer them.
